# Origin Tracking

Origin tracking records where materials are sourced, processed, and shipped before reaching buyers. The feature improves supply transparency and supports procurement teams managing region-specific sourcing restrictions.

## Source Visibility

Each product batch is connected to its originating [[suppliers]], manufacturing site, and transport path. Tracking records help validate sourcing claims alongside [[certifications]] and support audits required by [[compliance]].

## Operational Benefits

- Route mapping with [[logistics]]
- Delay analysis connected to [[delays]]
- Regional risk scoring through [[risk-management]]
- Shipment verification during [[checkout-flow]]

Origin insights are surfaced through [[analytics-and-reporting]] so buyers can compare reliability across sourcing regions and diversify procurement strategies.
