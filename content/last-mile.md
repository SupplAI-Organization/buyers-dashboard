# Last Mile

Last-mile fulfillment covers the final delivery stage between regional storage facilities and buyer destinations. The process focuses on delivery reliability, proof of receipt, and scheduling accuracy.

## Delivery Coordination

Last-mile operations depend on upstream routing from [[logistics]]. Delivery timelines are adjusted when transport interruptions are detected in [[delays]].

## Operational Constraints

- Urban delivery congestion
- Time-window scheduling
- Buyer receiving confirmation
- Route updates from [[ai-assistant]]
