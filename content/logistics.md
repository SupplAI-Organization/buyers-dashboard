# Logistics

Logistics coordinates the movement of raw materials between suppliers, warehouses, ports, and buyers. The logistics layer combines shipment scheduling, carrier coordination, and inventory routing to maintain predictable fulfillment across procurement operations.

## Fulfillment Coordination

Shipment planning depends on inventory readiness from [[suppliers]] and routing options defined in [[shipping-modes]]. Storage availability from [[warehousing]] influences delivery timelines, especially during seasonal demand spikes. Logistics data also supports checkout validation inside [[checkout-flow]].

## Tracking and Risk Handling

- Delay prediction using [[analytics-and-reporting]]
- Escalation workflows tied to [[risk-management]]
- Route optimization recommendations from [[ai-assistant]]
- Cross-border compliance checks through [[compliance]]
- Shipment origin verification from [[origin-tracking]]
