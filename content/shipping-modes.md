# Shipping Modes

Shipping modes define how raw materials move across domestic and international routes. Buyers and suppliers select transport methods based on cost, urgency, material sensitivity, and regional availability.

## Transport Categories

The platform supports road freight, rail transport, air cargo, and maritime shipping. Each mode integrates with [[logistics]] scheduling and warehouse allocation managed in [[warehousing]]. Shipment restrictions are validated through [[compliance]] before dispatch.

## Decision Factors

- Cost comparison from [[pricing-engine]]
- Delay probability linked to [[delays]]
- Regional sourcing analysis from [[origin-tracking]]
- Buyer urgency signals from [[buyer-personas]]
