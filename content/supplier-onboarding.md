# Supplier Onboarding

Supplier onboarding standardizes how new manufacturers and distributors enter the SupplAI platform. The process validates legal identity, production capability, banking information, and inventory readiness before suppliers become searchable in the marketplace. Onboarding workflows reduce procurement delays and improve downstream matching accuracy.

## Verification Workflow

The onboarding flow starts with profile creation and business verification through [[compliance]]. Suppliers then upload operational certifications linked with [[certifications]] and provide sourcing details tracked in [[origin-tracking]]. Initial quality benchmarks are defined alongside [[quality-control]] teams before the account is approved.

## Activation Stages

- Legal and tax verification
- Production capacity assessment
- Shipping capability review with [[logistics]]
- Pricing baseline setup inside [[pricing-engine]]
- AI-assisted profile enrichment using [[ai-assistant]]

The onboarding pipeline also prepares suppliers for dispute escalation rules defined in [[risk-management]].
