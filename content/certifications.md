# Certifications

Certifications validate supplier eligibility, product standards, and regulatory compliance across industries. The platform stores certification records centrally so procurement teams can verify authenticity during sourcing and negotiation.

## Certification Categories

Common records include ISO standards, export licenses, environmental declarations, and industry-specific approvals. These documents are attached to [[suppliers]] during [[supplier-onboarding]] and become part of ongoing [[compliance]] reviews.

## Usage Across the Platform

- Certification expiry tracking through [[analytics-and-reporting]]
- Automated reminders generated by [[ai-assistant]]
- Quality verification alongside [[quality-control]]
- Cross-border validation for [[shipping-modes]]

Certification status also contributes to supplier scoring inside [[pricing-engine]], especially when buyers prioritize low-risk sourcing partners.
