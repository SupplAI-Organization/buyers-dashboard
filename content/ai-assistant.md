# AI Assistant

The AI assistant provides workflow guidance, sourcing recommendations, and operational insights across the SupplAI marketplace. It acts as a coordination layer between procurement, logistics, and analytics systems.

## Intelligent Recommendations

The assistant evaluates supplier reliability from [[suppliers]], transport conditions from [[logistics]], and dynamic cost calculations from [[pricing-engine]]. Procurement teams also receive negotiation guidance connected to [[negotiation]] and risk alerts generated through [[risk-management]].
