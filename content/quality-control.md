# Quality Control

Quality control ensures raw materials meet contractual and regulatory standards before shipment or warehousing. SupplAI tracks inspection events, sample approvals, and batch-level defects to reduce procurement risk for buyers handling industrial materials.

## Inspection Process

Inspection schedules are tied to production batches from [[suppliers]]. Verification data is linked with [[certifications]] records so procurement teams can confirm standards across regions. Quality events are also connected to [[origin-tracking]] to identify recurring issues tied to sourcing locations.

## Monitoring and Escalation

- Batch inspections before dispatch
- Random warehouse sampling in [[warehousing]]
- Defect trend reporting in [[analytics-and-reporting]]
- Shipment hold procedures during [[delays]]

Repeated failures automatically increase monitoring scores within [[risk-management]]. Procurement managers can review inspection summaries before final approval during [[checkout-flow]].
