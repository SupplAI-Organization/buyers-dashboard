# Warehousing

Warehousing manages inventory storage, batch handling, and dispatch readiness across the marketplace network. Storage operations ensure procurement orders remain available for staged shipments and bulk purchasing.

## Inventory Handling

Warehouse records are synchronized with [[logistics]] schedules and supplier inventory feeds from [[suppliers]]. Quality checks from [[quality-control]] are performed before batches are approved for dispatch.

## Storage Operations

- Allocation planning for [[shipping-modes]]
- Inventory aging analysis in [[analytics-and-reporting]]
- Escalation support during [[delays]]
- Cost forecasting through [[pricing-engine]]
